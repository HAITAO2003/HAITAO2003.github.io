AI & RL Resources

Essential resources for learning artificial intelligence and reinforcement learning.

Books

Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction

Sutton & Barto

The definitive RL textbook covering fundamental concepts, algorithms, and mathematical foundations. Essential reading for anyone serious about understanding reinforcement learning.

Free online

Deep Learning

Goodfellow, Bengio & Courville

Comprehensive introduction to deep learning methods that are essential for modern RL. Covers neural networks, optimization, and advanced architectures.

Free online

Courses

CS285: Deep Reinforcement Learning (Berkeley)

Sergey Levine

Graduate-level deep RL course covering policy gradients, Q-learning, model-based RL, and advanced topics. Includes lectures, assignments, and projects.

Course website

OpenAI Spinning Up

OpenAI

Educational resource with key deep RL algorithm implementations, clear explanations, and practical coding exercises to get you started.

Spinning Up

Key Papers

Playing Atari with Deep Reinforcement Learning

Mnih et al. (2013)

The DQN paper that started deep RL by showing how neural networks can learn to play Atari games directly from pixels.

arXiv

Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms

Schulman et al. (2017)

PPO, a standard policy gradient method that balances sample efficiency with implementation simplicity and stability.

arXiv

Tools & Libraries

Stable Baselines3

PyTorch Implementation

Reliable RL algorithm implementations with consistent APIs, extensive documentation, and built-in tensorboard integration.

Documentation

OpenAI Gym

OpenAI

Standard toolkit for RL environments providing a unified interface for training and testing RL algorithms across diverse domains.
