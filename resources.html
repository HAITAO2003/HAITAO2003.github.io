AI & RL Resources

Essential resources for learning artificial intelligence and reinforcement learning

Books

Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction

Sutton & Barto

The definitive textbook on reinforcement learning. Covers fundamental concepts from tabular methods to function approximation with clarity and depth.

Read Online

Deep Learning

Goodfellow, Bengio & Courville

Essential foundation for modern RL. Comprehensive coverage of neural networks, optimization, and deep learning architectures.

Read Online

Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning

Christopher Bishop

Excellent treatment of probabilistic approaches to machine learning. Particularly strong on Bayesian methods and graphical models.

Learn More

Courses

CS285: Deep Reinforcement Learning

UC Berkeley - Sergey Levine

Graduate-level course covering modern deep RL algorithms. Includes lectures on policy gradients, Q-learning, model-based RL, and exploration.

Course Website

OpenAI Spinning Up

OpenAI

Educational resource with implementations of key deep RL algorithms. Excellent introduction with clear explanations and working code.

Visit Spinning Up

CS234: Reinforcement Learning

Stanford - Emma Brunskill

Comprehensive introduction to RL theory and practice. Covers bandits, MDPs, function approximation, and policy search methods.

Course Website

Key Papers

Playing Atari with Deep Reinforcement Learning

Mnih et al. (2013)

The DQN paper that started the deep RL revolution. Demonstrated learning to play Atari games directly from pixels using neural networks.

Read on arXiv

Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms

Schulman et al. (2017)

PPO has become the default policy gradient method due to its simplicity and effectiveness. Essential reading for modern RL practitioners.

Read on arXiv

Soft Actor-Critic

Haarnoja et al. (2018)

State-of-the-art off-policy algorithm for continuous control. Combines maximum entropy RL with actor-critic methods for stable learning.

Read on arXiv

Tools & Libraries

Stable Baselines3

DLR-RM

Reliable implementations of RL algorithms in PyTorch. Well-documented, tested, and actively maintained. Perfect for research and applications.

Documentation

Gymnasium

Farama Foundation

The maintained fork of OpenAI Gym. Standard toolkit for developing and comparing reinforcement learning algorithms with diverse environments.

Documentation

Ray RLlib

Anyscale

Scalable RL library with distributed training support. Includes implementations of many algorithms and supports custom environments.
